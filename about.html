About Us

Tsnat Kbrom From East Africa (Eritrea) and raised in Sudan. where i was introduce to the culture with so much similarity to mine. but with alot of difference as well. I loved drowing and doing all kind of art and one of the things i loved doing was Henna. Beautifull designesthat reflect the culture of many differnt people from all around the world.


alifornia based Henna designers organizing various henna parties on all celebrations making our guests enjoy the parties to the fullest. Our service is provided in San Francisco all over the bay. We professionally started our art work in September 2014 in Fremont California. We have 9 years of professional experience.
